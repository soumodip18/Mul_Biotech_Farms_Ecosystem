Proprietary Framework

The IRBAS™ operating
framework

Six integrated stacks developed from six years of field operations at Unit Nasibpur (2019–2024), forming a unified environmental intelligence and implementation system.

01
IRBAS-RESTORE™
Ecosystem restoration, soil bioremediation, land-use rehabilitation, and biodiversity recovery protocols. Addresses degraded land at landscape scale.
02
IRBAS-BIOENERGY™
Biomass valorization, circular bioeconomy design, and renewable feedstock systems tailored for rural agricultural landscapes.
03
IRBAS-HYDROGRID™
Integrated aquaculture systems, water resource management, hydroponics infrastructure design, and aquaponics engineering.
04
IRBAS-CARBON™
End-to-end carbon project development, sequestration measurement, MRV protocol design, and structured market linkage.
05
IRBAS-GEOSPATIAL™
Satellite monitoring, GIS decision support, remote sensing analytics, land-use change mapping, and climate intelligence overlays.
06
IRBAS-RURAL™
Farmer capacity building, FPO linkages, rural livelihood programming, and community-based climate adaptation at the grassroots.

Frequently Asked

Common questions

What is the IRBAS™ framework?

IRBAS™ (Integrated Regenerative Bio-Agri System) is the ecosystem's proprietary operating framework combining six stacks — RESTORE, BIOENERGY, HYDROGRID, CARBON, GEOSPATIAL, and RURAL — built from six years of field operations at Unit Nasibpur (2019–2024). It unifies biological systems, agricultural engineering, environmental science, and digital intelligence into a single decision-support architecture.

What services does Mul Biotech Farms offer?

Mul Biotech Farms offers services across agriculture (hydroponics, aquaponics, mushroom technology, spirulina, precision agriculture), climate & environment (carbon farming, ESG, climate risk assessment, biodiversity planning), engineering (DPR, feasibility studies, project design), and digital solutions (GIS, AI agricultural intelligence, environmental dashboards, dMRV platforms).

How are the three ecosystem entities related?

Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited is the flagship registered commercial entity. Mul Biotech Digital Farm Solutions is its MSME digital technology branch. SequestraBionix Foundation is a Section 8 NGO functioning as the CSR and carbon credit vehicle operated under Mul Biotech Farms. All three entities serve the flagship's growth and mission.

Where is Mul Biotech Farms based and who can collaborate?

Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited is based in Rishra, Hooghly, West Bengal, India. The ecosystem welcomes collaboration with government agencies, universities, research institutions, NGOs, climate organizations, CSR programs, investors, technology partners, agricultural enterprises, and startups working toward sustainable agriculture and climate action.
