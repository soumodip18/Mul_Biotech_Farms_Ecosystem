# Mul Biotech Farms Ecosystem
## Master Design & Development Specification v1.0

**Purpose:** This document is the single source of truth for designing, developing and maintaining the Mul Biotech Farms Ecosystem.

---

# 1. Vision

The Mul Biotech Farms Ecosystem is the official **research, technology, documentation and innovation platform** of Mul Biotech Farms Private Limited.

It complements the corporate website rather than replacing it.

- **Corporate Website:** Business, services, enquiries, company profile.
- **Ecosystem:** Technical knowledge, repositories, dashboards, research, publications, frameworks and innovation.

## Primary Objectives
- Demonstrate technical credibility.
- Centralize digital assets.
- Document research and innovation.
- Enable collaboration with government, academia and industry.
- Showcase execution through transparent documentation.

# 15. Final Development Brief

Build a premium GitHub Pages experience.

Requirements:
- Responsive
- Modern
- Interactive
- Fast
- SEO optimized
- Production ready

The homepage (index.html) should function as a digital ecosystem dashboard rather than a marketing landing page. It should immediately communicate that Mul Biotech Farms combines biotechnology, engineering, digital intelligence and sustainability into one integrated ecosystem.
